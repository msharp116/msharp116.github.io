Contacts

This page is here to help you keep track of any family, friends, enemies, organizations, etc your character may know or be a part of, as well as any new ones they may encounter during the course of their adventure. This table is just a general guide meant to aid you, you can feel free to fill is as much or little as you like.

The leftmost column tracks the name of the person or organization, the column to its left is for explaining the relationship between the contact and your character, such as sibling, friend, or enemy. The next column to the right is intented to have a brief desciption of the contact, such as what position they are in, where they live, or what they look like. The last column is for any aditional notes about them you may want to keep track of, such as if they were recently injured or are currently traveling with the party. If you need more rows, just click the "Add Row" button above. If you would like to remove a row, click on the red "X" icon to that appears to one the far right of the row.
